*"In everything, commit yourself to excellence and integrity." - P. F. Roysdon*

For more than 25 years, Dr. Roysdon has worked in engineering and applied mathematics, solving real-world problems.  His career spans academia, defense contractor, and federal government service.  Dr. Roysdon presently leads cutting-edge Cyber and Artificial Intelligence (AI) research at Leidos where he is Vice President, AI Chief Scientist, Technical Fellow, and Master-level Solution Architect.  Dr. Roysdon is also an adjunct professor at NIU.

Dr. Roysdon earned a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering (dual focus in applied mathematics & probability theory), from the University of California, as well as an M.S. in Aeronautical Engineering, M.S. in Electrical Engineering, M.S. in Mechanical Engineering, a B.S. in both Aeronautical & Mechanical Engineering, and completed graduate-level studies in theoretical physics.  He is a Senior Member of AIAA, Senior Member of IEEE, and served on the AIAA Aircraft Design Technical Committee for 15 years.

Dr. Roysdon has received numerous awards for innovations in artificial intelligence and machine learning (ML), applied to problems in the Intelligence Community, the Department of Defense, and the private sector, e.g., the National Security Agency (NSA) Inventor Award, NSA Director's Reserve Award, Office of the Director of National Intelligence Director's Award, Leidos Innovation in R&D Award, and twice received the Leidos CTO Award.

Dr. Roysdon has six patents-pending and he has published many peer-reviewed [books](http://www.roysdonfibonaccipress.com/), [scientific articles](https://github.com/pfroysdon/publications/tree/main/Papers), and [technical notes](https://github.com/pfroysdon/publications/tree/main/Tech_Notes).  His [research](https://pfroysdon.github.io/projects/) includes AI/ML, Offensive and Defensive Cyber, Cryptanalysis, Atomic and Nuclear Physics, Aircraft Design, Guidance, Navigation & Control (GNC), and RF signal analysis.  Recent research interests include Computational Biology (particularly with the application of AI/ML, e.g., AlphaFold) and Quantum Physics (again with AI/ML applications).

His other interests include [flying](https://youtu.be/8kgQ3hz9YNg) and [watchmaking](https://www.roysdonwatchco.com/).
