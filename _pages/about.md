For more than 25 years, Dr. Roysdon has worked in engineering and applied mathematics, solving real-world problems.  His career spans academia, private industry and federal government service.  Dr. Roysdon presently leads a cutting-edge artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) research team at a Fortune 500 company where he is a Vice President, AI/ML Chief Scientist, and a Technical Fellow.

Dr. Roysdon earned a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering (focus in applied mathematics & probability theory), from the University of California, as well as an M.S. in Aeronautical Engineering, M.S. in Electrical Engineering, M.S. in Mechanical Engineering, and B.S. in both Aeronautical & Mechanical Engineering. He is a Senior Member of AIAA, Senior Member of IEEE, and served on the AIAA Aircraft Design Technical Committee for 15 years.

Dr. Roysdon has published many peer-reviewed [scientific articles](https://github.com/pfroysdon/publications/tree/main/Papers), [books](http://www.roysdonfibonaccipress.com/), and [technical notes](https://github.com/pfroysdon/publications/tree/main/Math_Notes) in AI/ML, optimal state estimation, aircraft design, as well as guidance, navigation and control.

His outside interests include [watchmaking](https://www.roysdonwatchco.com/) and [flying aerobatics](https://youtu.be/8kgQ3hz9YNg) in a Pitts S2C.
